Managing Director

Dr. Obingo worked in pharmaceutical marketing for a period of 23 years, rising from Medical representative to a senior Manager in a leading Pharmaceutical company. Prior to establishing Radiance Pharmaceuticals, Dr. Obingo worked for Beximco Pharmaceuticals as one of the pioneer Medical Representatives in Kenya before moving to Glenmark Pharmaceuticals as the Institutional Sales Manager and then Dafra Pharma as the National Sales Manager. He is the Managing Director and head of Marketing Department at Radiance Pharmaceuticals Limited. He has vast knowledge of the market requirements and expectations derived from his field work as well as contacts established.

Marketing & Business

Marketing Strategy

Radiance focuses on brand marketing and positioning generics as affordable alternatives. The team works across Kenya with bases in Nairobi, Mombasa, Kisumu, Eldoret, and Mt. Kenya.

Institutional & Regional Business

We serve major institutions including KEMSA, Kenyatta National Hospital, MEDS, MTRH, MOD, Aga Khan Hospital, and Nairobi Hospital. Regionally, we supply markets in South Sudan, Rwanda, and DRC with vendor listings from UNDP.

Export & Documentation

Radiance maintains strong collaboration with the Pharmacy and Poisons Board and forwarding agents to streamline export approvals.

Training & Future Goals

Radiance offers professional training to bridge product knowledge gaps among medical professionals. Looking ahead, the company plans to expand its portfolio and eventually establish a manufacturing plant in Kenya.

Partners & Clients

We work with reputable suppliers such as ACI Healthcare Limited and serve hospitals, clinics, and healthcare institutions nationwide.

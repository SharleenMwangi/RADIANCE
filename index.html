About Us

Radiance Pharmaceuticals Limited was founded in 2006 by professionals with extensive experience in Kenya’s healthcare industry. We specialize in distributing high-quality, affordable products while providing training and consultancy services across the region. The Kenyan healthcare industry has developed over the years to be one of the most dynamic in the region. The need for high-quality, affordable products has led to increased importation of commodities mainly from Asian countries to meet the growing demand. Radiance has positioned itself as a leader in quality products in Kenya. We also engage in training and knowledge transfer to the medical fraternity through CMEs, consultancy services, and market information feedback.

Vision & Mission

  • OUR VISION: To emerge as a leading company in the region offering high quality and affordable products and services, thus remaining valuable partners to our clients and suppliers.
  • OUR MISSION: We are committed to being the premier company offering a reliable link between the manufacturers and the users. We apply ethical standards in acquiring, marketing, and distributing pharmaceutical products.

Delivery & Logistics

At Radiance Pharmaceuticals, we pride ourselves on ensuring timely and efficient delivery of all our products across Kenya.

For deliveries within Nairobi and its environs, our dedicated logistics team handles dispatch directly to hospitals, clinics, and pharmacies — guaranteeing reliability and care from our warehouse to your doorstep.

For deliveries to other parts of the country, we partner with reputable courier services such as Fargo Courier and G4S to ensure safe, traceable, and prompt transportation of all medical products to their destinations.

Whether near or far, Radiance ensures your supplies arrive on time and in perfect condition.

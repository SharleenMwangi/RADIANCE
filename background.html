Marketing & Business

We focus on brand marketing, positioning our products as affordable alternatives to original brands while maintaining exceptional quality. Our dedicated institutional representatives ensure effective follow-up on tenders, quotations, and LPOs. We operate across Kenya with bases in Nairobi, Mombasa, Kisumu, Eldoret, and Mt. Kenya.

Institutional Business

The institutional market commands up to 35% of Kenya’s pharmaceutical industry. We maintain strong presence in key institutions including KEMSA, Kenyatta National Hospital, MEDS, Moi Teaching and Referral Hospital, Ministry of Defence, and other leading hospitals.

Regional Business

Kenya’s strategic position enables us to expand regionally. We have established strong market links with South Sudan, Rwanda, and the DRC, and are enlisted as UNDP vendors supplying pharmaceutical products to Southern Sudan.

Export Business

Through close collaboration with the Pharmacy and Poisons Board and our forwarding agents, we ensure seamless export documentation and timely approval for overseas distribution.

Training & Consultancy

We address gaps in product knowledge and health affairs among medical professionals through structured training programs and consultancy services. Our expert-led sessions ensure compliance with training manuals and deliver quality outcomes within set timelines.
